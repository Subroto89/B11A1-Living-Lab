Available FAQ

How do you ensure the safety of pets during shipping?

Pets are placed in specially designed, secure, and comfortable carriers that ensure their well-being throughout the journey. We use temperature-controlled environments to keep pets comfortable, regardless of weather conditions.

How long does delivery take?

The delivery time can vary depending on a few factors, such as your location, the availability of the pet or product, and the shipping method chosen. Generally, here are some estimated delivery times:

Local Deliveries: If you're located within the same city or region as our shipping facility, deliveries typically take 1-3 business days.

Domestic Deliveries: For deliveries within the same country, it usually takes 3-7 business days.

How can I adopt a pet from your website?

Adopting a pet from our website is a straightforward and rewarding process. Here’s how you can go about it:

Browse Available Pets: Start by exploring our website's pet adoption section. You can filter pets by type, age, breed, and other preferences to find the perfect match for you.

Learn About the Pet: Each pet has a detailed profile with information about its temperament, health status, and background. Make sure to read through this information to ensure the pet is a good fit for your home and lifestyle.

Submit an Application: Once you've found a pet you'd like to adopt, click on the "Adopt" button to fill out an adoption application. Provide all the necessary information about yourself, your living situation, and your experience with pets.
